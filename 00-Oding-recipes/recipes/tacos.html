Tacos recipe

Description;

Tacos are a beloved Mexican street food that consist of soft or crispy tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients such as seasoned meats, fresh vegetables, cheese, and salsa. Whether simple or loaded, tacos are incredibly customizable and bring bold flavors with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Seasoned meat (like beef or chicken)
  • Salsa or pico de gallo

Steps

  1. Cook seasoned beef or chicken in a pan.
  2. Chop toppings like lettuce, tomato, and onions.
  3. Warm tortillas or taco shells.
  4. Fill with meat and toppings.

    5. Serve fresh with salsa or sauce.
