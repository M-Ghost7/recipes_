Lasgna recipe

Description;

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made by layering wide sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and a generous topping of melted mozzarella. Baked until golden and bubbling, it offers a satisfying blend of flavors and textures in every bite, making it a comfort food favorite.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef (or minced meat)
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef with garlic, onion, and tomato sauce.
  2. Boil lasagna sheets until al dente..
  3. Layer meat sauce, pasta, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes.
  5. Let it rest before serving.
