Pasta recipe

Description;

Pasta is a versatile staple of Italian cuisine, made from durum wheat and shaped into various forms like spaghetti, penne, or fusilli. Paired with sauces ranging from creamy Alfredo to spicy arrabbiata, it serves as a comforting and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne)
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Garlic or tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Sauté garlic and onions in oil or butter.
  3. Add sauce and mix in the pasta.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  5. Serve hot with cheese or bread.
